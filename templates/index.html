Boundary Task Risk

Tasks that live at the edge between two workstreams are the most common source of dropped balls. When Data hands off to Engineering, or Engineering hands off to Launch — neither team naturally owns that interface. A RACI-lite model makes it explicit: one person is Accountable per task, even at handoffs. Log every cross-workstream dependency in the RAID register with an owner and resolution SLA.
